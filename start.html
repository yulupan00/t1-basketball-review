Caption Review

Human Evaluation Study (T1)

In this study you will review … caption pairs. For each pair, compare the model's caption against the ground truth and assign a score (0-5) for each of seven alignment dimensions.

Scoring rubric (0-5 per dimension)
  • 0 — Dimension not applicable / cannot be judged from the captions.
  • 1 — Not aligned at all / contradicts the ground truth.
  • 2 — Largely misaligned; key facts missing or wrong.
  • 3 — Partially aligned; some correct, some incorrect.
  • 4 — Mostly aligned; minor issues only.
  • 5 — Fully aligned with the ground truth.
Dimensions you will score
  • Action accuracy — does the prediction describe the same actions?
  • Identity accuracy — same players / teams / entities?
  • Causality & outcome — does the result / cause-effect match?
  • Spatial understanding — locations / positions consistent?
  • Temporal understanding — timing / order of events consistent?
  • Contextual details — score, period, supporting facts consistent?
  • Final holistic score — overall alignment with the ground truth.
